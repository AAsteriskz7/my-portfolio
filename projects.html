Spoiler Alert

As the UI/UX Designer and Front-End Developer, I contributed to the design and development of Spoiler Alert!, a smart fridge platform that allows users to manage their groceries efficiently. Users can input foods and ingredients into a digital fridge, track expiration dates manually or via AI, and share their fridge with others. Additionally, the platform generates recipes based on available ingredients, prioritizing items nearing expiration.

LLMuminate

Inspired by the growing reliance on large language models (LLMs) like ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini, LLMuminate was developed to address the critical issue of misinformation in AI-generated content. LLMuminate is a Chrome extension designed to fact-check AI responses instantly against reputable, peer-reviewed journals like PubMed.
